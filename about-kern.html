About Kern
who we are
Our company’s current primary focus is to leverage the power of enzymes and cutting-edge software technology to create the world's most scalable and durable data storage infrastructure via synthetic DNA.

Based on his pioneering research on enzymatic DNA Synthesis, Dr. Henry Lee founded Kern Systems out of Church Lab at Harvard Medical School.
